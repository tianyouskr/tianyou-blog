Welcome to my blog! This is the first post.

## Why self-host a blog

- **You own the content** — posts are Markdown in a Git repo.
- **Free and zero-ops** — a static site on a CDN.
- **Fully customizable** — add whatever you like.

## Stack

1. [Astro](https://astro.build)
2. Keystatic for online editing
3. Pagefind for search
4. Giscus for comments
